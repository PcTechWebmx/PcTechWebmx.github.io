Ryzen 5600g

The AMD Ryzen 5 5600G and 5700G are here, and as always we bring you the review as soon as possible to see first-hand the news and, above all, its performance. We tested the smaller of the two, with 6 cores and 12 Zen 3 threads, alongside the most powerful 7-core Radeon Vega integrated graphics ever created.

Unlike the Ryzen 4000G APUs that only saw the light of day for OEM and Pro series, this new Cezanne architecture has been released for the benefit of everyone. A very versatile CPU at a good price that will be excellent in a multimedia PC, for work, and even for playing at a good level.
